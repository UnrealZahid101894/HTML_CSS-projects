How to Make the Best Coffee Ever!

Let's make some coffee

Ingredients

  • Coffee beans
  • Water
  • Milk (optional)
  • Sugar (optional)

Instructions

  1. Start by grinding the coffee beans to a medium-coarse consistency.
  2. Boil water and let it cool for a minute to reach the ideal brewing temperature (around 200°F or 93°C).
  3. Combine the coffee grounds and hot water in a French press or coffee maker.
  4. Let it steep for about 4 minutes, then press the plunger or filter the coffee. Pour the brewed coffee into a cup and add milk if desired.
